N° 01The Process

Six steps, six weeks.

01

Choose your last

Begin with one of three Tester lasts: the Atelier (men's), Bloomsbury (women's), or NY (unisex). Or send measurements for a bespoke last — adds two weeks.

02

Pick a silhouette

Loafer, derby, sneaker, mule, boot. Each silhouette has been refined across a decade of seasons; you're choosing from production patterns.

03

Choose materials

Italian box-calf, English suede, French chèvre, deadstock house leathers. Lining in cabretta or unlined for warm climates.

04

Sole & welt

Goodyear-welted with a single, double, or storm welt; soles in oak-bark leather, Dainite rubber, or our own moulded cup.

05

Personalise

Initials inside the sock-liner. Brass eyelets or blind-stitched. A letter to the wearer if it's a gift — we hand-write it in ink.

06

Hand-finish & ship

Six weeks from order. Trial fitting in our showroom or by post — we'll remake at no charge if it isn't right.

N° 03Co-Labs

Four-handed work.

Each year we open the workshop to two outside designers — one in spring, one in autumn — for a small, named capsule. Past collaborators include the Atelier Studio, Linnaeus, and the Tobacco Dock archive. Sign up to be notified when the next call opens.
