Made-to-order

Custom pairs ship within 6 weeks of order — sometimes sooner, never later without a written note. Bespoke lasts add 2 weeks. Once your pair is finished, we use the rates above.

Customs & duties

UK and EU shipments are sent DDP — duty and tax included in the price you see. Other regions are DDU: customs charges, when they apply, are due at delivery and set by your local authority. We can't predict them in advance.

"We refuse to under-declare. The box says what's inside. If you'd prefer otherwise, we're not the right shop."

Tracking

A tracking number is emailed when your pair leaves the workshop. If it hasn't moved in 48 hours, write to hello@tester.io — we'll chase the courier ourselves.

Packaging

  • — Two cotton dust bags per pair, drawstring, undyed.
  • — Acid-free tissue, never branded plastic.
  • — A short card listing the leather, last, and the day your pair was finished.
