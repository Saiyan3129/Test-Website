Topic 01

Orders & Shipping

How long until my pair ships?
Stock pairs ship the next Tuesday or Friday after your order — typically within 3–5 days. Custom pairs take 6 weeks; bespoke lasts add 2 weeks. Tracking is sent the moment it leaves the workshop.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes — anywhere DHL or FedEx will go, plus a few places they won't. UK and EU shipments are sent DDP (no surprise charges); other regions are DDU. See Shipping for full rates.
What if I'm not in when it arrives?
Couriers leave a card and try the next day. After two attempts the parcel goes to your local depot. We can re-route to your office or a friendly neighbour — write to us before the second attempt.
Can I change my order after placing it?
Within an hour, yes — most pairs are picked from stock the morning after. After that, it depends; write to hello@tester.io and we'll see what we can do.

I'm between sizes — up or down?
Loafers: down. Sneakers: up. Mules and boots: up if your toes need room. We'll exchange free once if it's not right.
Do you do half sizes?
In the Ezra and Bloomsbury lines, yes (UK 6½ — 9½). Plus Court and the NY range are full sizes only. Wider lasts available by request — adds a week.
Can I send my measurements?
For made-to-order yes — we have a measurement chart we'll send through. Stand on the chart, mark heel and longest toe, photograph it. We've done this with customers in twelve countries; it works.

Topic 03

Care

How do I care for the leather?
A horsehair brush after every wear, a soft cloth for marks. Once a month, a thin layer of neutral cream (we send a tin with first orders). Avoid waterproofing sprays — they grey the colour over time.
What about suede?
Brush against the grain, then with the grain. Suede protector once at the start of life — that's enough. For wet-weather wear, the Dainite-soled version is built for it.
My soles are worn — can you re-sole?
Yes — for life. Half price for Atelier members; full price otherwise (still cheaper than buying a new pair). Send the pair back to the workshop; we re-sole and refinish the leather while it's there. 3–4 weeks turnaround.
Can I machine-wash the cotton dust bags?
Yes — cold, no bleach, line dry. They go a little softer each wash; that's fine.

Topic 04

Custom & Made-to-Order

What's the difference between custom and bespoke?
Custom = our patterns, your choices (silhouette, leather, sole, lining, initials). Bespoke = a pattern cut for your foot, from your measurements. Bespoke adds 2 weeks and ₱8,000.
Can I commission something not on the configurator?
Sometimes. Write to us — describe what you have in mind. If we can do it, we'll quote. If we can't, we'll tell you (and may suggest a better atelier for it).
Can I see a sample before committing?
Leather swatches: yes, we'll post a small set. Full samples in your size: only at a showroom or a trunk show. See Showrooms for dates.

Topic 05

Loyalty Programmes

How do tiers work?
Apprentice on first purchase. Atelier on three pairs in a year. House by invitation. Custom and made-to-order pairs count double. Tiers reset on your join-date anniversary.
I lost my brass card.
We'll re-cut it. Same number, ₱500 to cover the brass. Keep it in your wallet next time, or don't — the discount is on file regardless.
How do I get invited to House?
We invite quietly, usually after a custom commission and at least four pairs over two years. There are fifty members, ever. We don't post a wait list because we don't keep one.
Can I gift a tier?
Yes — Apprentice and Atelier. House cannot be gifted, by design. See Gifting below.

Topic 06

Gifting

Can I send a gift card?
Yes — denominations from ₱2,500 to ₱50,000. Hand-stamped on heavy paper, posted in a small envelope, or e-mailed if it's last-minute.
Will the box have a price tag?
No, never — gift or not. Order summary goes by email; the parcel travels quietly.
Can I add a hand-written note?
Yes. Tell us what to write at checkout. We use ink — no printers — so allow up to 60 characters.

Topic 07

The Workshop

Where are the shoes actually made?
All cutting and finishing in our Northampton workshop. Some uppers (Plus Court sneaker only) are stitched by a small shop in Manila. The Ezra, Wilde, Bloomsbury, and Atelier lines are entirely Northampton-made.
Can I visit?
Yes — by appointment. Wednesday to Friday at the Northampton workshop, or any day in Manila. Tour, fitting, leather selection, and (usually) a cup of tea. See Showrooms.
Are you hiring apprentices?
We take one or two a year. The opening is announced in our newsletter and not advertised elsewhere — sign up at the bottom of any page.
