HelpReturns & Exchanges

Not quite right? We'll make it right.

30-day return window, free exchanges, and lifetime re-soling at half price for Atelier-tier members. Custom pairs are remade at no charge if the fit isn't there.

N° 01The Process

Four steps.

01

Write to us

Send a note to returns@tester.io with your order number. We'll reply within one working day with a postage label and a small form.

02

Repack the pair

Use the original box if you can; any sturdy carton if not. Pair must be in unworn condition (try them indoors only — soles tell tales). Include the form.

03

Post it back

Use our prepaid label (free for first exchange — domestic) or your own carrier. We're at the addresses on the Showrooms page.

04

Refund or remake

Refunds processed within 5 working days of receipt. Exchanges ship within 3 days; if the new size is out of stock we'll cut a fresh pair (no extra charge).

N° 02The Fine Print

The terms.

Return window
30 days from delivery. We use the courier's confirmed-delivered date, not the day the parcel was opened.
Condition
Unworn outdoors. Mid-soles cleaned. Original dust bags returned. We're not strict about the box but please use one.
Refunds
To original payment method. Card refunds typically appear within 5–10 days; banks differ.
Exchanges
First exchange is free domestically. Second exchange — actual postage, charged at cost.
Custom & made-to-order
Cannot be returned for refund (cut to your specification). Will be remade if the fit is wrong — at no charge — once.
Sale items
Final sale items (marked at checkout) are not returnable. We mention this clearly on the product page; nothing is hidden in the small print.
Re-soling
Half price for life — Atelier members. Full price (still less than the leather costs) for non-members. Posted to the workshop, returned in 3–4 weeks.
Faulty pairs
Out of warranty? Send a photograph anyway. Most things we can put right at the workshop. We'd rather repair than refund.
