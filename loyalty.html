N° 02How it works

Quietly, by hand.

Earning

Every pair counts toward your tier. Hand-finished pairs (custom or made-to-order) count double. Tiers reset annually on your join-date.

Cards

Apprentice — heavy-stock paper, blind-stamped. Atelier — etched brass. House — one of fifty. Members carry the same number for life.

Atelier visits

Once a year, by appointment, at the Northampton workshop or Manila showroom. Lasting demonstration, leather selection, and a fitting.

Privacy

No tracking pixels, no quarterly newsletters you didn't ask for. We send three letters a year. Read them or don't.
